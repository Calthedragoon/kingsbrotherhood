King's Brotherhood

Welcome to the King's Brotherhood! Here you can view some of the cool scenery and action that the game Final Fantasy XV brings to the table! This game has a lot different areas to explore and different monsters and daemons of varying strength, to give a challenge for any player.

The screenshots you see on this site were taken either throughout the game, or through the PS4's built in screenshot system.

Prince Noctis Lucis Caelum | Heir to the Lucis throne

Noctis is the main character of Final Fantasy XV, here's a good shot of Noctis protraying some nice casual wear!

Being blessed with the power of the Lucian Kings, Noctis wields a lot of power in battle. But he is always followed by his four bodyguards, but they are more like brothers than bodyguards.

Prompto and Gladio | Noctis's Bodyguards

Prompto Argentum is Noctic's teenage friend, and his bodyguard. He fights with pistols and firearms, and takes all of the great pictures of Final Fantasy XV!

Gladiolus Amicitia is a childhood friend of Noctis, and acts as shield to him. He will not hesitate to throw himself in the line of fire for the young prince.

Action Scenes from Final Fantasy XV

Here's Deadeye! Deadeye is a powerful behemoth that has been terrorizing all the chocobos in the area. Poor guys! Now looks like he's stalking Gladio, or maybe Gladio is stalking him!

Noctis always heads into battle prepared for any enemy, with sword in hand. Ignis fights with daggers and will always be there to protect the group and help them strategize to victory.

Flan! These are some of the biggest pests in Final Fantasy history! Magic is really the only thing that can damage them. You aren't gonna put a dent in them like that Noctis!
