Prompto, Gladio and Ignis | Noctis's Companions

Prompto and Noctis

Prompto Argentum is Noctis's teenage friend, and his bodyguard. He fights with pistols and firearms, and takes all of the great pictures of Final Fantasy XV!

Gladiolus and Noctis

Gladiolus Amicitia is a childhood friend of Noctis, and acts as shield to him. He will not hesitate to throw himself in the line of fire for the young prince.

Ignis and Noctis

Ignis Scientia is another childhood friend of Noctis, and acts as advisor to Noctis. He drives the crews royal vehicle, the Regalia, and cooks every meal for the group at camp!
